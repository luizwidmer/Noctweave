CLIENT PRIVACY POLICY
-----------------------------------------------------------------
Noctyra stores selected profile data locally on your device and transmits encrypted envelopes through relays you configure.
Even with end-to-end encryption, relay operators and network observers may infer metadata such as timing, source IP, destination relay, online status, and traffic volume patterns.
You are solely responsible for selecting trustworthy relays, hardening your devices, controlling backups, and evaluating metadata exposure in your threat model.
No developer-hosted relay infrastructure is provided.
